# Pricing and Licensing

## Licensing Model

- Product: ArchiFlow Desktop
- License type: Per seat, annual subscription
- Activation: License key / account-based activation (implementation policy dependent)
- Included updates: Minor and patch releases during active subscription

## Suggested Plans

| Plan | Target | Seats | Annual Price (USD) | Key Limits |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| Starter | Individual professionals | 1 | 99 | Single user, standard support |
| Team | Small teams | 5 | 399 | Shared onboarding, email support |
| Business | Multi-team operations | 20 | 1299 | Priority support, policy templates |
| Enterprise | Regulated/large orgs | Custom | Custom | SLA, procurement/compliance support |

## Positioning Notes

- Core value is risk reduction + operational time savings.
- Emphasize safe defaults (quarantine, dry-run, undo).
- Use ROI calculator (`roi-calculator-example.csv`) in discovery calls.
